# Retail Business Overview

## Objective
This document explains the retail business context used in the Databricks Retail Project.

## Retail Business Flow
1. Customer places an order
2. Order contains multiple products
3. Payment and shipment are processed
4. Data is generated at each step
5. Data is analyzed for business decisions

## Importance of Data in Retail
Retail organizations use data to:
- Track revenue and sales trends
- Identify top-selling products
- Understand customer behavior
- Improve inventory planning

## Types of Analytics
- **Descriptive:** What happened?
- **Diagnostic:** Why did it happen?
- **Predictive:** What will happen?
- **Prescriptive:** What action to take?

## Outcome
This understanding will drive:
- Data model design
- ETL pipelines
- Analytics and dashboards
